For Project Owners

Reach the right
investors, discreetly

We connect serious, pre-qualified investors with development projects across Croatia. No public listings, no unwanted exposure — just the right introduction at the right time.

Why List With Us

What we offer project owners

Access to Qualified Investors
Our investor network includes private individuals, family offices and institutional funds from Croatia, Austria, Germany, Switzerland, the UK and beyond — all pre-verified before introduction.
Full Discretion
Your exact location, identity and documentation remain private until we have verified investor interest and both parties are ready to proceed. No public exposure without your consent.
International Reach
Croatia Investments actively promotes to investors across 12+ countries. Your project gains visibility in markets actively seeking Adriatic and CEE real estate opportunities.
No Upfront Costs
We operate on a success fee model. You pay nothing to list your project — our fee is a percentage of the transaction value, only upon successful completion of the deal.
Professional Presentation
We prepare and present your project professionally — curating the right level of information to attract serious interest without revealing sensitive details prematurely.
Dedicated Support
From initial listing to final introduction, our team guides you through the entire process and remains available to answer questions from both sides throughout.
Listing Process

How to list your project

01
Initial Contact
Reach out via our contact form or email. We schedule a confidential consultation to understand your project and goals.
02
Documentation Review
Our team reviews your project documentation, permits and ownership structure. We verify everything before proceeding.
03
Agreement & Listing
We sign a posrednički ugovor defining terms, exclusivity period and success fee. Your project goes live within 5 business days.
04
Investor Introduction
When a qualified investor expresses interest, we facilitate a direct introduction and provide them with your full documentation package.
Listing Requirements

What we need from you

To maintain the quality and credibility of our platform, we require the following documentation before listing any project.

  • ✓
    Proof of OwnershipZemljišnoknjižni izvadak (Land Registry extract) confirming legal ownership of the property or development land.
  • ✓
    Conceptual Design / Preliminary ProjectArchitectural plans or development concept showing intended use, scale and design direction.
  • ✓
    Permit Status DocumentationCurrent status of all permits — lokacijska dozvola, građevinska dozvola or uporabna dozvola as applicable.
  • ✓
    Investment OverviewApproximate total investment required, expected return, and preferred deal structure (full acquisition, JV, equity).
  • ✓
    Identity VerificationValid ID or company registration documents for the project owner or authorised representative.
