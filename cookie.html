Cookie Policy

This policy explains what cookies are, which cookies Croatia Investments uses, and how you can manage your preferences at any time.

Last updated: March 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information on their own, but they may be linked to personal data we hold about you.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them). They can be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services embedded in that website.

Cookies We Use

We use a minimal set of cookies, grouped into two categories:

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
ci-cookie-consent Essential Stores your cookie preference so you are not asked again on every visit. 12 months

_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics cookies used to distinguish users and measure site usage in aggregate. Set only with your consent. 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics cookie used to distinguish users across sessions. Set only with your consent. 24 hours

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They enable core features such as security, session management, and your cookie consent preferences. These cookies cannot be disabled — without them, the website cannot operate correctly.

We do not use essential cookies to track you or to serve advertising.

Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website in aggregate — for example, which pages are most visited, how long users stay, and from which countries visitors arrive. This information helps us improve the site.

Analytics data is anonymised and aggregated. We do not use it to identify individual visitors. You can opt out at any time using the preference controls below, or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or profiling purposes.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website does not embed third-party social media widgets, advertising networks, or tracking pixels that set cookies independently. The only third-party cookies we may set (with your consent) are Google Analytics cookies, governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Browser-Level Controls
